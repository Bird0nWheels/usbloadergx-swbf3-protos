Tool download notice
====================

This script can download two third-party command-line tools, both
GPL-2-or-later:

  * rvthtool v2.0.1 -- David Korth / GerbilSoft.
    https://stuff.gerbilsoft.com/.rvt/rvthtool_2.0.1-win64.zip
    (~12 MB)

  * wit (Wiimms ISO Tool) v3.05a r8638 cygwin64 -- Dirk Clemens / Wiimm.
    https://wit.wiimm.de/download/wit-v3.05a-r8638-cygwin64.zip
    (~10 MB)

If either is already on your PATH, it will be detected and the
corresponding download is skipped.

Downloads are unpacked into the local "tools\" subfolder next to this
script and invoked as separate processes. They are not modified,
repackaged, or redistributed by this project. License text travels
inside the downloads.

Type YES to proceed, anything else to cancel.
